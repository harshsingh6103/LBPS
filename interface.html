2 Channel Relay PCBA

Key Features

  • 2 channel 10 AMP Relay board.
  • Controls 2 loads of up to 10 amps each at 120V or 240V AC.
  • Suitable for lighting, holiday displays etc.
  • Fully assembled and electrically tested.
  • LED indicator for each relay.
  • Can be controlled directly by Microcontroller or NI Card.

Product Description

The 2 Channel 12V Relay Module (Low level Trigger) can be controlled by interface devices like Arduino and other microcontrollers. Each channel features 3 separate terminals: NO (Normal Open), COM (Common), and NC (Normal Closed). Power must be a 12DC voltage.

Interface & Power Details

  • **Power Input:** 12V (Positive), GND- (Negative).
  • **Interface:** CH1 (Trigger), CH2 (Trigger), GND (Isolated), VCC 5V (Optional).
